# 🛡️ NjangiSafe
**Digitalizing Trust in Cameroonian Tontines.**

## 🌟 What is NjangiSafe?
In Cameroon, a **Njangi** (tontine) is a traditional way for friends and family to save money together. Every month, everyone contributes a fixed amount, and one person takes the whole "pot."

**The Problem:** Sometimes, a person takes the pot early in the cycle and stops contributing, leaving the other members at a loss. This is a major issue of trust and financial fraud.

**The Solution:** **NjangiSafe** is a mobile platform that acts as a "Digital Notary." It automates the collection of money and uses a smart security rule to ensure everyone stays honest until the very end.

---

## 🎯 The Goal
The mission of NjangiSafe is to move tontines from cash-based, risky manual processes to a **secure, transparent, and automated digital system** using Mobile Money (MTN/Orange).

---

## 🛠️ How It Works (The "Secret Sauce")
NjangiSafe uses a unique **60/40 Security Rule**:
1. **The Payout:** When it's your turn to win, the system sends **60%** of the money directly to your Mobile Money account.
2. **The Safety Net (Escrow):** The remaining **40%** is kept in a "Digital Vault" (Escrow).
3. **The Guarantee:** This 40% is only released or credited back to you as you continue to pay your future contributions. This ensures that even after you "win," you are financially motivated to keep supporting the group.

---

## 💻 Technical Stack (The Tools We Use)
For the tech-curious, here is what powers NjangiSafe:
* **Database:** MongoDB (To store group history and savings records).
* **Server:** Node.js (The brain that calculates the 60/40 split).
* **Payments:** Integrated with MTN Mobile Money APIs.
* **Architecture:** MVC Pattern (Organized like a professional bank system).

---

## ✅ Advantages
* **Anti-Fraud:** The 40% hold-back makes it nearly impossible for someone to "run away" with the money.
* **Transparency:** Every member can see exactly who has paid and how much is in the vault.
* **Convenience:** No need to carry large bags of cash; everything happens on your phone.
* **Automatic Records:** No more manual notebooks; the system tracks every franc.

---

## ⚠️ Current Limitations
* **Digital Literacy:** Users must be comfortable using a smartphone and Mobile Money.
* **Internet Access:** A stable connection is required to process transactions.
* **Regulatory Compliance:** As a fintech project, it requires official partnerships with telecommunication operators and financial regulators for large-scale use.
